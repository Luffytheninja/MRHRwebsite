Research & Surveys. Evidence-Backed Decisions

At the heart of our mission is evidence-based action. We conduct in-depth research to identify the root causes of maternal mortality, from gaps in healthcare access to delays in emergency response. Our findings inform national policies, guide community-based interventions, and drive   innovations in maternal care. By turning data into solutions, we help ensure safer pregnancies and healthier outcomes for mothers in Nigeria and Africa.

Direct Interventions End-to-End Support

Direct Interventions– MRH’s direct interventions aim to improve maternal and reproductive health in underserved communities. Our approach combines community-based programming, health education, and training of health workers to ensure safe pregnancies, skilled deliveries, and healthy mothers and babies.

Advocating Change Leading the Charge

We work to reduce maternal mortality in low-income and marginalised communities by promoting access to quality maternal and reproductive healthcare. We raise awareness, influence policy, and drive behaviour change through community engagement, media campaigns, and strategic partnerships. By mobilizing resources and empowering at-risk communities, we help women have safer pregnancies and healthier lives.
